Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2023

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, language preferences, and learning goals.

Voice Data: We collect audio recordings of your speech for pronunciation analysis. These recordings are processed to provide feedback and improve our AI models.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our platform, including lesson progress, practice sessions, and feature usage.

Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system, browser type, and IP address for security and optimization purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • • To provide and improve our AI-powered language learning services
  • • To personalize your learning experience and track progress
  • • To develop and improve our speech recognition algorithms
  • • To communicate with you about service updates and new features
  • • To ensure platform security and prevent fraudulent activities

3. Voice Data Processing

Your voice recordings are processed using advanced AI algorithms to analyze pronunciation patterns. We take the following measures to protect your voice data:

  • • All voice data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • • Recordings are processed anonymously for AI training
  • • You can delete your voice recordings at any time
  • • We never share individual voice recordings with third parties

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • • Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • • Access your personal data
  • • Correct inaccurate data
  • • Request deletion of your data
  • • Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@Vāṇī.com

6. Children's Privacy

Vāṇī is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on our website and, if appropriate, through other communication channels. Your continued use of Vāṇī after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of those changes.
